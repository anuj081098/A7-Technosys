Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get a quote?

We provide quotes within 1-2 hours during business hours. For complex projects, we may need up to 24 hours to provide detailed pricing and recommendations.

What information do I need to provide for a quote?

Basic requirements include PCB specifications (layers, size, quantity), timeline, and any special requirements. For design services, sharing your schematic or concept helps us provide accurate quotes.

Do you sign NDAs for confidential projects?

Absolutely! We understand the importance of confidentiality in electronics development. We're happy to sign NDAs before discussing your project details.

Can I visit your facility?
