Our Journey: From Vision to Industry Leadership

A7 Technosys began its journey in 2013 as Welkin Design, with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide affordable, precise, and rapid PCB design services to India's growing electronics industry. What started as a small design consultancy focused on helping startups and innovators quickly evolved into something much bigger.

By 2015, recognizing the need for integrated manufacturing solutions, we expanded our capabilities and rebranded to A7 Technosys. This transformation marked our evolution from a design house to a comprehensive PCB manufacturing and assembly facility, offering end-to-end solutions under one roof.

Today, more than a decade later, we stand as one of India's most trusted PCB manufacturers, serving over 500 clients ranging from innovative startups to established OEMs across automotive, LED lighting, IoT, and industrial sectors.
