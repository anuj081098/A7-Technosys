How to Use the Embedded Gerber Viewer

  1. Locate your PCB manufacturing ZIP (export from EDA: KiCad, Altium, Eagle, EasyEDA, etc.).
  2. Open the viewer area below; drag & drop your ZIP or individual Gerber files into the Tracespace interface.
  3. Toggle layers (copper, mask, silkscreen, drill) to visually verify alignment, polarity, reference designators and outline.
  4. Check drill hits and pad clearances; confirm no floating copper or missing mask openings.
  5. Once satisfied, click “Get Quotation” above to request fabrication & assembly pricing (include layer count, finish, quantity).
Privacy Notice: Your files are handled directly by the embedded third-party viewer in your browser session. A7 Technosys does not intercept or store Gerber contents. Close your tab to clear session state.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is any of my PCB data stored or uploaded?

No. All processing happens client-side inside your browser memory. When you refresh, data is gone.

Which Gerber formats are supported?

Standard RS-274X Gerber, NC drill (.DRL/.XLN) plus typical layer naming for copper (GTL/GBL), mask (GTS/GBS), silkscreen (GTO/GBO), paste (GTP/GBP), outline (GKO) & mechanical (GM1).

How do I request a manufacturing quotation?

Use the "Get Quotation" button at the top – submit core specs (layer count, thickness, finish). We'll respond rapidly.

Can you assist with PCB design improvements?

Do you offer turnkey assembly?
