Everything you need to know about our PCB manufacturing services

We offer the fastest PCB prototyping in India with sample boards ready in just 24 hours for standard 2-layer designs. Complex multilayer boards typically take 48-72 hours. Rush orders can be accommodated with our express service.

We work with FR4, CEM1, Rogers, and metal core substrates. We handle single to 8-layer boards as standard, with custom stackups available. Thickness from 0.4mm to 6mm, various copper weights, and all major surface finishes including HASL, ENIG, and OSP.

We offer complete end-to-end services including PCB design from scratch, design optimization, manufacturing, assembly, and testing. Our experienced engineers can take your project from concept to finished product.

We are ISO 9001:2015 certified with comprehensive quality management systems. All PCBs undergo 100% electrical testing, automated optical inspection (AOI), and thorough quality checks before shipment.
