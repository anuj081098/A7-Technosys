Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a PCB design project?

Simply email your schematic, specifications, or even a hand-drawn concept to our team. We'll review your requirements and provide a detailed consultation with recommendations and pricing.

What design software do you use?

We work with all major EDA platforms including Altium Designer, KiCad, Eagle, and OrCAD. We can work with your preferred format or recommend the best tool for your project.

Do you really offer unlimited revisions?

Yes! We believe in getting your design perfect. Our unlimited revision policy ensures you're completely satisfied with the final design before we deliver the files.

Can you handle complex multilayer designs?

Absolutely! Our experienced team regularly designs complex multilayer PCBs up to 8 layers with high-speed signals, controlled impedance, and advanced routing techniques.

What files will I receive upon completion?

You'll receive a complete design package including Gerber files, drill files, pick and place files, BOM, assembly drawings, and 3D renderings - everything needed for manufacturing.
